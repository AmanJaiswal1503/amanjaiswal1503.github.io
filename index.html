Projects

A brief information on the interesting projects that I have worked on!

Video Steganography

Guide: Dr. Aditya Nigam | IIT Mandi
  • Constructed a two-staged pipeline for full video steganography, hiding one video file into another video of same resolution.
  • Extracted frames from videos and used two 3D-CNN networks for hiding and revealing task. Learning spatio-temporal features helped hiding the information in temporal domain considering the redundancy in video frames.
  • The developed model, combined hiding network and revealing network, was trained end-to-end to minimize the reconstruction loss.

AutoClean: Automated Data Cleaning

Guide: Mr. Vitobha Munigala, Dr. Kedar K. | IBM Research Labs, IIT Dharwad
  • Working to develop a tool for holistic data cleaning to improve the quality of data, thereby, increasing the performance of underlying machine learning model.
  • Created a taxonomy of different data quality problems that could exist in data and negatively affect the performance of ML model.
  • Developing a tool to create a pipeline of data cleaning operations, extracted from different data cleaning libraries, that should be applied to free the data of errors and inconsistencies in the most efficient and beneficial way.
